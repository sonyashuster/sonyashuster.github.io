Places I've Lived

While I've only ever had one "home", I've lived in a total of nine "dwellings" in three cities/towns in two provinces. I'm going to show them to you.

"Home" (aka Toronto)

I grew up in Toronto, near High Park. I moved back home almost two years ago and have lived there ever since. High Park is the largest park entirely within the city of Toronto. Among other things, it has an epic castle playground and multiple ponds with lots of ducks, geese, and other wildlife; there's even a small zoo.
